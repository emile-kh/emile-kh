Émile Khafizov


Émile is a digital artist who working with different types of animation: from cut-out and hand-drawn to 3d and with combination of these techniques with ai animation.


The main themes of Émile’s artworks are endangered animals and the ultimate nature of things. His works are inspired by classical cinema, space exploration, garage music, and Japanese poetry.


Émile was studied as an aircraft designer. He was a photographer for 10+ years with awards and participated in international exhibitions in Japan, China, Sweden, and Russia. He made a few short films while was a student in film school which was at a local film festival. Also, Émile working as a documentary film editor.
